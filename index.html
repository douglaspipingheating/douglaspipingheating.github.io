About Us

Douglas Piping and Heating is family own business, with over 20 years experienced in residential and commercial services. With a strong dedication and commitment to our customer's we provide new installs or repairs to your existing equipment. Call us for all your piping and heating needs. We provide free estimates and look forward in providing your residence or business with quality service.

Our Services

We provide the following services for both commercial and private properties:

General Plumbing Needs:

  • Bathroom plumbing: We repair and replace showers, toilets, sinks, and all of the connected pipes and drains.
  • Kitchen plumbing: We can install and repair sink faucets and plumbing lines for dishwashers and other appliances.
  • Heating: We repair, replace, or install Heaters & Water Tanks and Plumbing
  • We also provide plumbing inspections

Specialties:

- Our team specializes in steam, oil, and gas hot water heaters, both tankless and conventional. We'll provide the reliable service you need.
